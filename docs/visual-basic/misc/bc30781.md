<span data-ttu-id="223de-103">`Continue` 문이 `Do`루프, `For`루프 또는 `While`루프 내에 표시되는지에 따라 `Continue` 문 뒤에는 `Do...Loop` , `For...Next` 또는 `While...End While` 이 와야 합니다.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="223de-103">A `Continue` statement must be followed by `Do`, `For`, or `While`, depending on if the `Continue` statement appears within a `Do...Loop` loop, `For...Next` loop, or `While...End While` loop.</span></span>  
  
 <span data-ttu-id="223de-104">**오류 ID:** BC30781</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="223de-104">**Error ID:** BC30781</span></span>
